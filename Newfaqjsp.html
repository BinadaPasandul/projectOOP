Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about bidding, payments, and account management.

General Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the 'Login' button in the top right corner and select 'Create Account'. You'll need to provide a valid email address and set up a password.
Is there a fee to join Bidify?
No, creating an account and browsing auctions is completely free. We only charge a small commission fee on successful bids.

Bidding & Payments

How does the bidding process work?
Once you find an item you want, enter your maximum bid. Our system will automatically bid for you up to your maximum amount. You'll be notified if you're outbid.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and direct bank transfers. All transactions are securely processed through SSL encryption.
