General Questions

How do I create an account?

Getting started is simple! Click the 'Login' button in the top right corner and select 'Create Account'. You'll need to provide a valid email address and set up a secure password. We recommend using a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters for maximum security.

Is there a fee to join Bidify?

Bidify offers free membership with full access to all auctions. We only charge a minimal 3% commission fee on successful bids. Premium membership options with enhanced features are available for power users.

Bidding & Payments

How does the bidding process work?

Our smart bidding system allows you to set your maximum bid amount. We'll automatically increment your bid to maintain your position up to your specified limit. You'll receive real-time notifications via email and SMS if you're outbid.

  • • Proxy bidding system
  • • Bid increment protection
  • • 24/7 auction monitoring
